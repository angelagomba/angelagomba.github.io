Adidas ad campaign: Feel the Music

One of my assignments for this class was to do an ad campaign for a company. The company I chose to create an ad campaign for was Adidas, making a good excuse for a fashion shoot. I brought together two of my friends, both fans of Adidas, and we took to the streets of downtown Santa Monica. In this photo, Nathaly is posed by a brick building in an alley. At first, she was a bit shy and unsure of how to pose, but once she started having fun with it, I captured this photo of her dancing.
