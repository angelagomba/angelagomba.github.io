Photography to me.

I am as amateur as they come. Although I have taken 2 photography courses where I learned how to develop my own film, what it meant to change your ISO settings, and how to use Photoshop, my skills in photography is nothing special. Despite my lack in professional skill, this portfolio is my life. I think of friends and family, and I see the photos I've taken of them. I despair over heartbreak and loss, and I hold onto the past in a picture. I wonder about the strangers I've photographed, and how just mere milliseconds of a snapshot have brought life to my work. This is my life, photography to me.
