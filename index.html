Welcome to my personal website. Here you will find portfolios and some of my coding projects (coming soon!).

I will be continuing my musical adventure in a band as one of their guitarist.

I've done some writing for Storybench by Northeastern's School of Journalism, which is a website that explores digital storytelling through various software tools. I also occasionally write poetry, and am currently working on a book of short stories.
